About AYUR
“To ensure good health: eat lightly, breathe deeply, live moderately, cultivate cheerfulness, and maintain an interest in life.” – William Londen .

The Ayur Health-Care System is a new, self-contained product, that replaces the current system of fitness apps that are unintuitive, not interactive, i.e. no real-time feedback to the users. Our main aim is to make people realize the importance of staying healthy and fit during the pandemic. Implement a digital health e-wallet application that keeps a track of various features related to diet plans, health expenses, workout routines etc. This product is a website intended to make fitness journeys intuitive, interactive, and free.
